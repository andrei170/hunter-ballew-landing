FAQ

Common questions, before applying.

How is this different from Make It Count?

Make It Count is the philosophy. The Playbook is the install. Same five elements, but turned into modules, scripts, templates, and SOPs you can run on a Monday. If you've read the book and want to operate differently - this is the next step.

How fast will I see results?

Most operators install module 1 (pricing) within the first 7 days of enrolment and report margin lift on the next 5-10 quotes they write. The Friday-afternoon firefighting cycle takes longer to break - usually inside 4-6 weeks of the leadership-rhythm module going live. The system is designed so the first wins happen fast and compound from there.

Is this Revolt?

No. Revolt is the high-ticket mastermind for $5M+ roofing operators and is by application. The Playbook is the self-paced course for $1M-$5M operators that sits below it. Many Playbook graduates apply to Revolt 6-12 months later.

What's the time commitment?

3-4 hours per week for 12 weeks. Most module video is async; one Live Q&A with Hunter the first Tuesday of every month. Recordings if you can't make it live.

What's the price?

$497 for the standard tier (full curriculum, monthly Q&A, lifetime access). $997 for the personal-review tier (everything in standard plus a personal video review of your roofing company by Hunter). Both tiers, one-time payment.

What if I'm a $5M+ roofer? Is this still for me?

The Playbook is built for $1M-$5M operators - if you're past $5M, you'll still get value from modules 5 and 6 (leadership rhythm + scale-or-sell), but the on-ramp problems we're solving are mostly behind you. Apply to Revolt instead. The Playbook acceptance form will route you there if you indicate $5M+ revenue.

Refund policy?

Yes. Finish the first two modules, decide it's not for you, full refund within 14 days of enrolment. No questions.

Will the templates work for my market?

Yes. The templates are revenue-stage-specific, not market-specific. Tampa, Dallas, Buffalo, anywhere - if you're $1M-$5M roofing, the systems install.

Why do I have to apply?

You don't have to. The application form is four minutes and it just helps me size cohorts and route the live Q&A topics to what's actually live in your shop. Submit the form, you get straight to enrolment - no setter calls, no back-and-forth.
